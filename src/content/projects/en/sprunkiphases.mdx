# Sprunki Phases

[Sprunki Phases](https://www.sprunkiphases.com/) is a browser-based music-creation game where you drag and drop quirky characters onto the stage, each adding its own beat, melody, or effect, to build your own track. It collects every phase of the viral Sprunki series in one place, playable free with no download.

## Key Features

- **All Phases in One Place** - Play every entry from Phase 1 through the latest Phase 17 "Horror Edition"
- **Drag-and-Drop Music Creation** - Layer characters to mix beats, vocals, melodies, and effects in real time
- **Distinct Editions** - From the original classic to dark, neon, and horror-themed remixes, each phase has its own sound and style
- **Interactive Soundboard** - Preview samples (beat, vox, monster, scream, melody, and more) before you build
- **Community Mods** - Browse thousands of custom sound packs created and shared by the community
- **Free & Instant** - Runs right in the browser, no install required

## What Makes It Different

A single hub for the entire Sprunki phenomenon — every remix and edition, ready to play instantly, with a community library that keeps growing.
