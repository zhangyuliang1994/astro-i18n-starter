# CupCities

[CupCities](https://cupcities.com/) is an independent fan information hub for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, covering all 16 host cities across the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Built by fans, for fans — not affiliated with FIFA or the official organizers.

## Key Features

- **Daily Matchday Info** - Kickoff times, TV/streaming channels, and full match schedules
- **Host City Guides** - Stadiums, fan zones, airport-to-venue routes, and accommodation tips
- **Fan Zone Finder** - Public viewing areas, food options, and family zones across host cities
- **Stadium Guides** - Capacity, seating, parking, bag policies, and accessibility info
- **Travel Planning** - Airport routes, parking strategies, ticket-safety advice, and viewing options
- **Broadcast Info** - Coverage across FOX, Telemundo, and streaming platforms
- **Matchday Alerts** - Daily email briefings in six languages

## Coverage

All 104 matches, 16 stadiums, and complete travel infrastructure — fact-checked against official sources, with an emphasis on accessibility and real-world matchday logistics.
