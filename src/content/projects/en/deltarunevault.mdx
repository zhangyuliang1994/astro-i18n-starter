# Deltarune Vault

[Deltarune Vault](https://deltarunevault.com) is an unofficial fan site for *Deltarune*, Toby Fox's indie RPG. Instead of a prose-heavy wiki, it focuses on practical, interactive tools you can use while you play.

## Key Features

- **Boss Cheat Sheet** - Searchable table of every boss across Chapters 1–5, filterable by chapter/route with tactics and strategy notes
- **Character Database** - A page per character with appearances, relationships, quotes, and trivia
- **Interactive Tools** - Character quizzes, trivia games, damage calculators, and decision helpers
- **Walkthroughs** - Complete guides for Chapters 1–4, with Chapter 5 content launching alongside the game

## What Makes It Different

Built for mid-gameplay utility rather than reference reading — filter bosses, compare stats, and find what you need without scrolling a wall of prose.
