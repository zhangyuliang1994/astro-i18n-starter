Blog AI Knowledge Loop - Editorial Cover

Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: bilingual technical blog cover

STRUCTURE: A clean left-to-right circular knowledge loop, visually balanced and centered.

ZONES:
- Left: two stacked document cards with small code-line marks and one clear label, “MDX”.
- Center-left: Git branching symbol inside a rounded automation tile, small label “Actions”.
- Center: compact edge-computing node with cloud outline, small label “Worker”.
- Center-right: glowing but flat geometric vector index made of connected dots, small label “Vectorize”.
- Right: friendly chat bubble containing a concise answer and two tiny citation cards, no paragraph text.
- A returning arrow from the article cards to the answer visually completes the knowledge loop.

LABELS: MDX, Actions, Worker, Vectorize, AI
COLORS: Deep navy (#10233F) background; electric blue (#3B82F6) for request flow; teal (#14B8A6) for indexed knowledge; purple (#8B5CF6) for AI; white (#F8FAFC) cards; amber (#F59E0B) only for citations.
STYLE: Clean flat vector illustration with bold geometric shapes, crisp outlines, subtle depth, no photorealism, no logos, no watermark. Minimal technical editorial cover. Text must be large, exact, and limited to the five labels.
COMPOSITION: Generous margins and negative space, no busy background, no decorative code rain.
ASPECT: 16:9, high clarity, suitable as a blog cover.
